Project Overview

Hello! We are two students studying at Claremont Graduate Univeristy and this application was built as a part of one of our GIS classes there. Scotch Journey is an application that allows you to experience the world of Scotch whisky in new and exciting ways. The goal was to build a small, portable application that allows you to interact with our ArcGIS-powered map to understand the world of Scotch. We've also gone through and added additional information including a brief history, regional differences, and a small guide on how best to delve into the world of Scotch.

Objectives

  • Create a small, portable, effective web application
  • Tie into ArcGIS Online via the ArcGIS for JavaScript API
  • Display a map (or maps) helping to give an overview of Scotch whisky
  • Provide the visitor with additional information aimed at helping them on their very own Scotch Journey
